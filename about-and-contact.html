About and Contact

I'm Nora, an artist and graphic designer from China. This website exhibits a selection of my past projects. My practice can be found across the areas of generative art, branding and conventional graphic design. I'm interested in the matter of image generation through creative coding.
I hold a bachelor's degree in Business Administration (2016) from Communication University of China, a postgraduate diploma in Design for Visual Communications (merit, 2018) and a master's degree in Graphic Media Design (merit, 2020) from London College of Communication, University of the Arts London (UAL). I'm currently working as a freelancer.
