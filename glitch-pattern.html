Pattern images generated with z-fighting glitch.

Glitch Pattern

Z-fighting is a phenomenon in 3D rendering which occurs when two or more layers are positioned in identical or proximate positions. Layers ‘fight’ with each other to be shown on screen pixels and result in a flickering rasterisation effect.
With various configurations and utilisation this computer glitch as a graphic generator, the images allow us to have a glimpse of the digital world of 0s and 1s.
