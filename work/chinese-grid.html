可同时生成方形和圆形风格简体中文字形的几何网格系统。

Chinese Grid

中文字形网格

△ Click on the components to construt a figure of your choice and have two generated outcomes.
△ 点击部件以组建您想要的文字或图形，由系统生成两种结果。

Format: interactive webpage, printed poster (A1)
Scope: generative typography, grid construction and digitalisation, poster design
Year: 2018
Type: independent project
In terms of making a new Chinese font, traditionally, designers shall construct and adjust each character manually with pre-designed component strokes. Unlike English where there are only a small number of alphabets and symbols, the Chinese character making process is to be repeated thousands of times due to its massive amount.
This project aims at challenging the traditional method of Chinese type design with a systematic approach, where a "toolkit (base grid)" is provided instead for anyone to make the characters they need in the way they prefer.

形式：交互式网页、纸质海报（A1）
范畴：生成式字体、网格构建与数字化、海报设计
时间：2018年
类型：独立项目
通常而言，制作一套中文字体需要由设计师根据基本笔划部件进行对每个文字的手动拼装和调整。然而由于汉字字符数目巨大，这个过程通要重复成千上万次，消耗巨大的人力和时间成本。
通过建立一个几何网格组件化的系统，本项目旨在探索字体设计的新方法，使设计师甚至普通人能够根据自己的偏好自行组装和运用所需字符。

△ Each point and line has its corresponding part in the system, which allows the simultaneous generation of a character in two styles.
△ 每个点和线段在系统中都有其对应的部分，在这种机制下可同时生成两种风格样式的字符。
△ Posters which exhibit the design of the grid system by having the square and circular grids overlapped.
△ 海报通过重叠方形和圆形网格以展示网格系统设计。
△ Application of the grid system on a piece of text (excerpt from "Short Song Style" by Cao Cao).
△ 该网格系统在一段诗文上的应用（节选自曹操的《短歌行》）。
