A geometric grid system which generates Simplified Chinese characters in both square and circular styles.

可同时生成方形和圆形风格简体中文字形的几何网格系统。

Chinese Grid

中文字形网格

Click on the components to construt a figure of your choice and have two generated outcomes.
点击部件以组建您想要的文字或图形，由系统生成两种结果。

In terms of making a new Chinese font, traditionally, designers shall construct and adjust each character manually with pre-designed component strokes. Unlike English where there are only a small number of alphabets and symbols, the Chinese character making process is to be repeated for thousands of times due to its massive amount.
This project aims at challenging the traditional method of Chinese type design with a systematic approach, where a "toolkit (base grid)" is provided instead for anyone to make the characters they need in the way they prefer.

产出一套中文字体的传统方法往往是由设计师根据基本笔画一笔一划地手动拼写和调整每个文字。因为汉字字符数目巨大，这个过程通常要重复成千上万次。通过建立一个几何化的体系，本项目旨在探索和挑战传统的字体设计方法。

Each point and line has its corresponding part in the system, which allows the simultaneous generation of a character in two styles.
每个点和线段在系统中都有其对应的部分，在这种机制下可同时生成两种风格样式的字符。
