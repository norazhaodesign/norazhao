书籍与小册子

Machine Translation

机器翻译

This booklet aims to visualise the influence of machine translation by comparing a sample text and its translation iteration side by side with information highlighted.

本手册旨在通过将示例文本及其迭代的机器翻译进行横向比较，用几种颜色突出显示的文本所传达信息间的差异，以可视化机器翻译带来的影响。

The Letter Evolution

字母演化论

This booklet aims to demonstrate how pattern making techniques can be used to generate designs of typography and the matter of legibility.
The letter "e", being the mostly used letter in English together with many other languages, was chosen for demonstration. Futura was applied due to its geometric form.

该手册旨在演示图案制作技术如何用于生成字体设计及其可识别性的问题。
字母“e”作为英语以及许多其他语言中最常用的字母，在实验中被选中用于个例演示。Futura则是因其几何的设计在此作为字母“e”的字体。
