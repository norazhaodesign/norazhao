几何展示

△ Overview of the poster set.
△ 本组海报概貌。

This set of posters aim at promoting a hypothetical exhibition about visual language and grammar at Kemistry Gallery, London.
Each poster mianly shows pattern, shape and line in turn to reflect the theme of this event. Some elements go over the edges to achieve coherence.

这组海报旨为伦敦的Kemistry画廊推广有关视觉语言和语法的虚构展览。
每张海报的分别依次展示图案、形状和线条以反映本次活动的主题。一些元素会越过边缘以实现海报之间的连贯性。
